<p ><a href="neural.html" >Neural Networks</a>A freeware program for training and running neural networks.  Uses the lms, backpropagation, and an experimental version of the minimum disturbance principle.  The program was designed to aid in experimentation.  The number of nuerons is only limited by memory.  Choose activation functions: linear, threshold,tanh,and logistic.  The activation may vary accross layers.  </p>
